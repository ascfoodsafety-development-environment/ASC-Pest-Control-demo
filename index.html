Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

Which pests do you handle?

Cockroaches (German & American), rodents (rats & mice), flies, ants, termites, bed bugs, fleas, mosquitoes, stored-product insects (moths, beetles), pantry pests, spiders and occasional invaders.

How quickly can you respond?

We prioritise urgent infestations and compliance-driven sites, with rapid response across Gqeberha and the surrounding Eastern Cape. Call 061 138 3989 or WhatsApp us for the soonest available slot.

Are your treatments safe for family, staff and food areas?

Yes. Our Integrated Pest Management approach uses targeted, registered products applied by trained technicians, with methods aligned to food-safety and infection-prevention standards.

How should I prepare for a treatment?

We'll give you simple, specific prep steps when you book — typically clearing access to affected areas. For food sites we coordinate around your production schedule.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance plans?

Yes — monthly, bi-monthly, quarterly and annual plans with monitoring, trend reporting and service certificates, plus priority response between visits.

What is Integrated Pest Management (IPM)?

IPM is a science-led approach combining inspection, sanitation advice, proofing, monitoring devices and targeted treatment — controlling pests with the least necessary intervention.

Can you support our HACCP / BRCGS / FSSC 22000 audit?

Absolutely. We provide site maps, numbered devices, trend reports, corrective-action logs, service certificates and threshold-based escalation — the documentation auditors expect.

What are the signs of termites?

Hollow-sounding wood, mud tubes along walls or foundations, discarded wings and tight-fitting doors. If you notice these, book an inspection promptly to limit damage.

Which areas do you cover?

Gqeberha and the wider Eastern Cape — including Kariega, Despatch, Jeffrey's Bay, Humansdorp, Addo, Port Alfred, Makhanda, Coega and surrounding industrial hubs.
